SUPPORT & INFORMATION

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about delivery, warranty coverage, payments, and ordering.

You can determine your delivery fee through the order customization in the shop. Enter your province and city so we can calculate the shipping cost accurately.

You can contact our customer support team directly through our provided phone numbers so we can assist you with your order and delivery concerns personally.

Ovens, bread slicers, and rollers come with a 1-year service warranty covering motor and mechanical components under normal use conditions.

Mixers come with a 7-day motor replacement warranty and a 1-year motor service warranty. Some wear-and-tear products may not include warranty coverage.

You may request changes or cancellations within 24 hours after placing your order, depending on processing status and equipment availability.

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) and Bank Transfer for eligible orders nationwide.

OUR STORY

BUILT THROUGH GENERATIONS

From a humble family business in 1975 to a growing bakery equipment brand trusted nationwide.

1975

Our Humble Beginnings

Jose So founded the company with a vision to provide quality baking equipment to Filipino bakers and businesses.

JSVL

Jose So Vic & Lillian Corp.

The second generation carried on the family legacy and expanded operations while maintaining dedication to reliable bakery equipment.

ZGENS

Zosimo, Gian, Edward, Nenita, and Sheryl Corp.

The third generation revitalized the business while preserving the company’s strong family values and craftsmanship.

2018

Bake King Metal Fabrication Services

Gian rebranded the company into Bake King Metal Fabrication Services, modernizing operations while staying rooted in quality and reliability.

2022

Kalamunding, Camarines Norte Branch

Bake King expanded to the Bicol region through its Kalamunding branch, bringing trusted baking equipment closer to more Filipino bakers.
