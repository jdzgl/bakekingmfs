ABOUT US

1975

OUR HUMBLE BEGINNINGS

It all started in 1975 when Jose So founded the company with a vision to provide quality baking equipment to Filipino bakers and businesses. What began as a small operation grew steadily through dedication, hard work, and a passion for serving the baking industry.

JSVL

JOSE SO VIC AND LILIAN CORP.

As the business flourished, the second generation carried on the legacy by renaming the company to JSVL — Jose So Vic & Lilian Corp. This marked a new chapter of growth as the family expanded their commitment to delivering reliable baking equipment across the country.

ZGENS

ZOSIMO, GIAN, EDWARD, NENITA, AND SHERYL CORP.

The third generation breathed new life into the business, renaming it Z-Gens in honor of the family members who continued to uphold the company's values. Together, they strengthened the foundation built by their predecessors and expanded their reach even further.

2018

BAKE KING METAL FABRICATION SERVICES

In 2018, the fourth generation ushered in a fresh identity as Gian took the helm and rebranded the company to Bake King Metal Fabrication Services. Under his leadership, the company modernized its operations while staying true to its roots of crafting high-quality baking equipment.

2022

BAKE KING KALAMUNDING, CAMARINES NORTE BRANCH

In May 2022, Bake King expanded its presence by opening a new branch in Kalamunding, Camarines Norte, bringing its trusted baking equipment closer to the Bicol region. This milestone reflects the company's continuous growth and dedication to serving bakers across the Philippines.
