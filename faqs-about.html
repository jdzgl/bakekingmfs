WHAT IS THE WARRANTY COVERAGE FOR OVENS, BREAD SLICERS, AND ROLLERS?

Ovens, bread slicers, and rollers come with a 1-year service warranty that covers the motor and mechanical components under normal use. Please note that dents, holes, blades, cutting parts, and any components subject to regular wear and tear are not covered by the warranty. For warranty claims, please contact our support team with your proof of purchase and we will assist you accordingly.

WHAT IS THE WARRANTY COVERAGE FOR MIXERS AND OTHER EQUIPMENT LIKE BANKAS, STAINLESS TABLES, TRAY RACKS, AND CRUSHERS?

Mixers come with a 7-day motor replacement warranty from the date of delivery, along with a 1-year service warranty for motor-related concerns. Rollers also carry a 7-day motor replacement policy. For equipment such as bankas, stainless tables, tray racks, and crushers, these are considered wear-and-tear products and therefore come with no warranty. However, if you encounter any issues, feel free to message us on our Facebook page and we'll do our best to help you out.

CAN I MODIFY OR CANCEL MY ORDER AFTER IT HAS BEEN PLACED?

You may request changes or cancellations within 24 hours of placing your order by contacting our support team. Since baking equipment such as ovens, bread slicers, and dough rollers are often large or made-to-order items, orders that have already been processed or dispatched cannot be modified. Please double-check your specifications (size, voltage, capacity) before confirming your purchase.

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) and Bank Transfer as our payment methods. For bank transfer, payment details will be provided upon checkout. Please note that we do not offer installment payment options at this time. If you have any questions about your payment, don't hesitate to reach out to us.

ABOUT US

1975

OUR HUMBLE BEGINNINGS

It all started in 1975 when Jose So founded the company with a vision to provide quality baking equipment to Filipino bakers and businesses. What began as a small operation grew steadily through dedication, hard work, and a passion for serving the baking industry.

JSVL

JOSE SO VIC AND LILIAN CORP.

As the business flourished, the second generation carried on the legacy by renaming the company to JSVL — Jose So Vic & Lilian Corp. This marked a new chapter of growth as the family expanded their commitment to delivering reliable baking equipment across the country.

ZGENS

ZOSIMO, GIAN, EDWARD, NENITA, AND SHERYL CORP.

The third generation breathed new life into the business, renaming it Z-Gens in honor of the family members who continued to uphold the company's values. Together, Zosimo, Gian, Edward, Nenita, and Sheryl strengthened the foundation built by their predecessors and expanded their reach even further.

2018

BAKE KING METAL FABRICATION SERVICES

In 2018, the fourth generation ushered in a fresh identity as Gian took the helm and rebranded the company to Bake King Metal Fabrication Services. Under his leadership, the company modernized its operations while staying true to its roots of crafting high-quality baking equipment for every baker's needs.

2022

BAKE KING KALAMUNDING, CAMARINES NORTE BRANCH

In May 2022, Bake King expanded its presence by opening a new branch in Kalamunding, Camarines Norte, bringing its trusted baking equipment closer to the Bicol region. This milestone reflects the company's continuous growth and unwavering dedication to serving bakers across the Philippines.
